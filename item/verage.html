True Earth—Care for Your Skin Just Like Nature Would

Verage is a natural skin care solution, made exclusively with the natural, helpful benefits of plant extracts. Infused with the power of CPTG® essential oils, this system is based on the best that nature has to offer your skin. It consists of Veráge Cleanser, Toner, Moisturizer, and Immortelle Hydrating Serum.

  • CPTG essential oils
  • Scientifically proven plant extracts
  • Natural preservative system
  • Highly effective formulas
  • Amazing results

Verage Cleanser

Lifts away dirt, makeup, and impurities as it reaches deep into your pores to cleanse and purify. Healthy, smooth skin begins with Verage Cleanser. As the first step in your daily skin care regimen, Verage Cleanser leaves skin feeling clean and soft while its fresh, herbaceous aroma energizes the senses.

Verage Toner

Tightens, tones, and smooths your skin while reducing the appearance of pores. This second step in the Verage Skin Care Collection prepares skin for the rich emollients and nutrients found in Verage Hydrating Serum and Moisturizer. This cool, invigorating formula can be used as a refreshing mist throughout the day for a fresh, dewy complexion.

Verage Moisturizer

Provides an ideal moisture balance and reduces the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, resulting in a smooth, even complexion.

Verage Immortelle Hydrating Serum

Promotes youthful-looking, glowing complexion; improves skin barrier recovery function Experience the age-defying effects of Verage Immortelle Hydrating Serum. This serum contains our popular Immortelle essential oil blend and an ingredient complex called L22. L22 is a combination of jojoba, macadamia, and olive esters that mimics the skin’s surface lipids when at their optimum health and youth, typically around the age of 22.

This lipid complex promotes skin firmness and elasticity, especially in aging skin, and was proven to improve the recovery of skin barrier function. Veráge Immortelle Hydrating Serum is an innovative, highly effective formula that produces results you can see and feel, instantly.
