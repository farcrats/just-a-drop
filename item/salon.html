Root to Tip Serum Product Description

Natural CPTG Certified Pure Therapeutic Grade® essential oils can help maintain soft hair and a healthy-looking scalp. Complete with nourishing essential oils like Cedarwood, Lavender, Peppermint, Marjoram, and more, the doTERRA Salon Essentials Root to Tip Serum formula is designed to provide the hair with hydration and protection, without weighing hair down like other products do. The use of pure essential oils allows the Root to Tip Serum to gently nourish and rejuvenate the scalp while providing hair strands with proper moisture. This formula provides salon-level hair care without leaving behind a greasy residue—giving hair a light, healthy glow.

The doTERRA Salon Essentials line is full of purifying, soothing products designed to maintain soft, smooth, healthy-looking hair. doTERRA Salon Essentials Healthy Hold Glaze Product Description

While hairstyling products are designed to enhance the appearance of hair, they can often be tough on the scalp, leave behind a heavy residue, or even expose the hair to harmful chemicals. The doTERRA Salon Essentials Healthy Hold Glaze uses CPTG® essential oils to protect, enrich, moisturize, and soften the hair, no matter the length, color, or style. In addition to safe, natural ingredients, the Healthy Hold Glaze provides hair with a light, flexible hold, rather than weighing you down with a thick, oily residue. For beautifully styled hair, take advantage of the delightful aroma and gentle application of the doTERRA Salon Essentials Healthy Hold Glaze.
